Natural Language Processing

The computers cannot understand the language used by humans (like- English,Hindi,Japanese). So, the goal of NLP is to enable people and computers to communicate in a natural (humanly) language (such as English) rather than in a computer language.

OR


Natural language processing (NLP) is a subfield of linguistics, computer science, and artificial intelligence concerned with the interactions between computers and human language, in particular how to program computers to process and analyze large amounts of natural language data.


The field of NLP is divided in two categories:

  • Natural Language understanding
  • Natural Language generation
