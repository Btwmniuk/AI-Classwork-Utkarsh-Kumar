Data Science

Data can be defined as a unorganized record of a particular quantity. It is the different values of that quantity represented together in a set. It is a collection of facts and figures to be used for a specific purpose such as a survey or analysis. When we arrange data in an organized form, it can be called information.

Data is mainly of four types:

  1. Audio Data
  2. Video Data / Image Data
  3. Textual Data
  4. Big Data
